Using accounts

On this website, an account is only needed if you want to save the current state of the application. Without an account, the page will load in the same state every time; with an account, the page will load as you left it when you closed the page (or logged out).

Logging in

When you see the option to log in, simply type your email and password, and hit enter or the login button. If you don't have a account, click on create an account on the same screen. If you don't want to create an account, click SKIP; the only disadvantage is that your work won't be saved.

Creating an account

When you open the page, the login dialog allows you to create an account. If you skipped that, you can open the login dialog also by clicking on the account icon (the icon in the top row that looks like a pawn). To create an account, you'll need to fill in an email address and a password. The only restrictions are that the password must contain 8 characters or more, and that the email address is valid, and isn't already associated with an account.

Logging out

You don't have to log out, but the option is available under the account icon after logging in.

Privacy policy

We only use your email address for authentication and resetting your password.
